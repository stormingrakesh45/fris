Scoring Formula
Event Score = Category Min + Severity × ( Category Max – Category Min )
Example: Rating Downgrade (range 5–15), Severity = 0.9  →  5 + 0.9 × (15 – 5) = 14.0 ≈ 14 (ELEVATED)

Competitive Advantage

FRIS vs. General-Purpose AI

Purpose-built for financial risk intelligence — deterministic, auditable, and regulatory-grade, where general-purpose AI tools are broad, stateless, and opaque.

Attribute FRIS General-Purpose AI
Data Provenance Licensed: Refinitiv, SEC EDGAR, ASIC, FCA Crawled web data; lacks regulatory depth
Noise Reduction 77k → 34k unified events via deduplication Repetition bias skews risk perception
Scoring Logic Deterministic: Jaccard, volume, Decay Gates Probabilistic; prompt-sensitive outputs
Entity Persistence 100+ funds tracked in PostgreSQL over time Stateless; no long-term fund memory

Key takeaway: FRIS is purpose-built for financial risk intelligence which is deterministic, auditable, and regulatory-grade — whereas general-purpose AI tools are broad, stateless, and opaque.
